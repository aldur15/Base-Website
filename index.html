About Me

I'm a software developer and HCI researcher from Regensburg, Germany, with a strong focus on conversational AI, XR/VR environments, and modular system design. Over the past years, I've developed emotion-aware embodied agents in Unreal Engine, built scalable web backends using FastAPI and Docker, and collaborated closely with academic partners on interdisciplinary research.
